Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan

The founding father

Sheikh Zayed's Timeline:

  • 1918: Sheikh Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan is born in Abu Dhabi.
  • 1946: Appointed as the governor of Al Ain.
  • 1966: Became the Ruler of Abu Dhabi.
  • 1971: Played a crucial role in the founding of the United Arab Emirates and becomes the first President of the UAE
  • 1974: Oversees the signing of a border agreement with Saudi Arabia.
  • 1981: Helps establish the Gulf Cooperation Council (GCC).
  • 1996: Re-elected as President of the UAE for the fourth term.
  • 2004: Passed away on November 2nd.
